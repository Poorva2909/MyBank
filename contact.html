State Bank of India Customer Care

An account holder can call on MyBank 24/7 customer care number for any kind of questions, queries, feedback or complaint. The bank has 2 toll-free numbers for customer support:

  • 1800 11 2211
  • 1800 425 3800
  • 080-26599990​​

Credit Card Customer Care: MyBank credit card customers can call the bank’s dedicated credit card services department and get instant service related to their credit card. A customer can call the following MyBank credit card toll-free numbers:

  • 1860 180 1290
  • 1800 180 1290

MyBank Personal Loan Customer Care: Customers can call the MyBank personal loan customer care team to get answers to their queries regarding their loans and EMIs. One can place a call to the following toll-free number:

  • 1800-11-2211
  • 1800-425-3800
  • 080-26599990 (toll-number)
